Pasta Chips With Whipped Feta Dip

ABOUT:

Pasta chips have become a viral sensation, revolutionizing snack time with their crispy and satisfying crunch. They are made from thin slices of pasta that are fried or baked, offering a unique flavor that sets them apart from traditional potato chips. The popularity of pasta chips can be attributed to their simplicity, ease of preparation, and the variety of flavors and pairings available. From classic to seasoned and stuffed, pasta chips come in a wide range of shapes and sizes, making them a versatile snack for any occasion.


INGREDIENTS:

For The Pasta Chips

  1. 8 ounces rigatoni pasta
  2. 1 tablespoon olive oil
  3. 3 tablespoons freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  4. ½ teaspoon Italian seasoning
  5. ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  6. Kosher salt
  7. black pepper

For The Whipped Feta Dip

  1. 4 ounces crumbled feta cheese
  2. 2 ounces Greek yogurt
  3. 1 garlic clove
  4. 2 tablespoons olive oil

INSTRUCTIONS:

For The Pasta Chips

  1. Bring a large pot of heavily salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta al dente according to package directions and drain.
  2. Toss the pasta with olive oil, Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Air fry at 400°F for 12–15 minutes, tossing a couple of times.

For The Dip

  1. Add the crumbled feta to a food processor and pulse to create small crumbs. Add the Greek yogurt and garlic. Process while streaming in the olive oil until the feta is creamy, 4–5 minutes, scraping down the sides as necessary.
  2. Serve the pasta chips with the whipped feta dip.

Equipment

  1. Ceramic Nonstick Cookware Set
  2. Fine Mesh Sieve/Strainers
  3. Mixing Bowl Set
  4. Instant Vortex Plus Air Fryer with ClearCook
  5. Food Processor
  6. Measuring Cups and Spoons

Notes

  • Shake the air fryer basket a few times while cooking. It helps the pasta chips get crispy all over.
  • The pasta chips taste best fresh, but you can store them in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 5 days.
